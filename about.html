About Divya Sharma & Qualis-Pistorius:
Divya Sharma is a passionate and dedicated Computer Science Engineering (CSE) student currently in her third year at a prestigious institution in Alwar, Rajasthan. With a strong foundation in web development and problem-solving, she enjoys creating innovative and user-friendly digital experiences. Her expertise spans HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, with plans to expand into React to enhance interactivity and functionality. Divya’s love for technology and creativity drives her to build projects that are not only visually appealing but also serve a meaningful purpose. One of her most exciting projects is "Qualis-Pistorius," a bakery website that brings together the art of baking and the power of AI. This six-page website offers a delightful experience where users can explore exclusive collections, book baking classes, and even receive AI-powered personalized bakery recommendations based on their health preferences. The platform also features special event-based offerings, allowing users to order customized cakes for occasions like New Year, Diwali, and Valentine's Day. Additionally, it hosts baking competitions for schools, with winning recipes being officially launched under the winner’s name. Through Qualis-Pistorius, Divya aims to revolutionize the online bakery experience by blending tradition with technology, making it more interactive, accessible, and personalized for every user.
